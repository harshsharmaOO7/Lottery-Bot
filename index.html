❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What time is Lottery Sambad result published?
Lottery Sambad results are declared three times daily: 1:00 PM (morning), 6:00 PM (evening), and 8:00 PM (night). This page is updated automatically within minutes of each draw via our bot.
How to check Nagaland State Lottery Result?
After the draw time, the result image and official PDF appear automatically on this page. Click "View PDF Below" to preview in browser, or "Download PDF" to save the official result.
Is Nagaland State Lottery legal in India?
Yes. Nagaland State Lottery is 100% legal, regulated by the Government of Nagaland since 1972 under the Lotteries (Regulation) Act, 1998. It is one of India's oldest state-run lotteries.
How long does it take to claim lottery prize money?
After submitting all required documents along with the original winning lottery ticket, prize money is typically processed within 3 working days. Sign the back of your winning ticket immediately.
How is this website updated automatically?

About Nagaland State Lottery

Established in 1972, Nagaland State Lottery is regulated under the Lotteries (Regulation) Act, 1998. Each draw has a unique name based on the day of the week. The prize structure includes a First Prize of ₹1 Crore. All results on this page link to official government-issued PDFs.

About Kerala Lottery Result

Kerala Lottery, operated by the Government of Kerala since 1967, runs seven different weekly draws including Win-Win, Akshaya, Karunya Plus, Nirmal, and Pournami. Official results are published daily at 3:00 PM IST by the Kerala State Lotteries Department.

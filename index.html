Lottery Sambad is India's most popular state lottery, operated by the Government of Nagaland. Results are published three times every day — at 1:00 PM, 6:00 PM, and 8:00 PM IST. About Nagaland State Lottery

Established in 1972, Nagaland State Lottery is regulated by the Nagaland government under the Lotteries (Regulation) Act, 1998. Each draw uses a different name depending on the day of the week. The morning draw (1PM) is commonly called the "Dear Lottery" series, while evening and night draws follow the bird series naming convention.

How to Read Lottery Sambad Result

The official result is published as a PDF document and result image. Match your ticket number with the first prize, second prize, and consolation prizes listed. Prizes range from ₹100 to ₹1 crore depending on the draw and ticket number.

Kerala Lottery Result Today

Kerala Lottery, operated by the Government of Kerala since 1967, runs seven different weekly lotteries including Win-Win, Akshaya, Karunya Plus, Nirmal, and Pournami. Results are announced at 3:00 PM daily by the Kerala lottery department.
